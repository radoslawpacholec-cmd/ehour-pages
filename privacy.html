Privacy Policy for eHour Sidebar

The eHour Sidebar add-on does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. It only accesses Google Workspace data that is necessary for its core functionality, such as reading spreadsheet information to display project tracking info.

All access to user data is handled via secure OAuth 2.0 authentication and is never stored or transmitted to any external server.

For questions, please contact us at support@econsulting.pl.
